# Operating Model

Revenue Forecast Control Tower supports a planning workflow that joins commercial performance with operational signals.

## Core Views

- `forecast summary`
  Quarterly outlook, bookings attainment, forecast gap, and risk-adjusted coverage
- `regional forecast`
  Region-level performance, conversion outlook, and miss risk
- `pipeline risk`
  Opportunity health, stage slippage, and account concentration
- `alerts`
  High-risk accounts and regions requiring immediate follow-up
- `scenarios`
  Expected upside under conversion and renewal-improvement assumptions

## Typical Workflow

1. Generate or ingest fresh source signals.
2. Refresh curated tables and exported dashboard snapshots.
3. Review alerts and regional forecast gaps.
4. Use scenario endpoints to compare intervention options.
5. Route high-priority actions to sales operations, customer success, or commercial leadership workflows.
